Expolring NY NOW: A Global Pulse on Design & Decor

Gaining insights and inspiration from the world’s leading home and lifestyle fair.

New inspirations

Exploring NY NOW: A Global Pulse on Design & Décor

This March, Kansal Enterprises had the privilege of attending NY NOW, one of the world’s most celebrated marketplaces for home, lifestyle, and décor. The event brought together designers, creators, and global tastemakers, all under one roof to share their vision for the future of living spaces.



Walking through the vibrant aisles of NY NOW, we were immersed in a global dialogue of design — from artisanal craftsmanship rooted in tradition to cutting-edge contemporary innovations. Each interaction, each product, told a story of passion, sustainability, and cultural expression.


For Kansal Enterprises, this wasn’t just an exhibition; it was a pulse check on the industry. What trends are shaping the future of homes? How are global markets adapting to changing lifestyles? And most importantly, how can we continue to deliver décor and design solutions that resonate with people everywhere?


Our biggest takeaway was the convergence of heritage and modernity. Whether it was handcrafted pieces infused with cultural narratives or futuristic designs embracing new materials, the show reaffirmed that décor is not just about aesthetics — it’s about identity, storytelling, and experience.


At Kansal Enterprises, we see our role as more than just producers; we are curators of inspiration. Events like NY NOW push us to raise the bar, stay aligned with international trends, and continue to innovate with our materials, finishes, and artistry — from traditional metalwork to contemporary copper plating and UV-print innovations.


As we return from NY NOW, we carry not only new ideas but also a renewed commitment to bring world-class design into everyday living. Stay tuned as we translate these inspirations into our upcoming collections.

Join us on this journey of design and discovery.
